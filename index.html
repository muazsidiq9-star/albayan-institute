Frequently Asked Questions

Click on a question to view the answer.

Do I need any prior knowledge of Arabic? +

No. The program starts from absolute basics and gradually builds up to fluency.

Is this self-paced or live? +

It is structured learning. Depending on the program, you may have guided lessons and live support.

Will I be able to speak Arabic fluently? +

The system is designed to build comprehension first, then gradually develop speaking confidence.

How much time do I need daily? +

Even 20–40 minutes daily is enough if you stay consistent with the system.

Do I get support during learning? +

Yes, students are supported through structured guidance and community interaction.

Is this suitable for Qur'anic Arabic? +

Yes. The foundation helps you understand Arabic used in Qur'an and classical texts.
